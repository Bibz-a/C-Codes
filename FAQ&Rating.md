# FAQ & Rating System – Feature Overview

This document provides a brief overview of the **FAQ** and **Rating** pages developed for the website.

---

## FAQ Page

The FAQ page is designed to provide clear and reliable information, managed through moderator control.

### Features
- Moderators can **add new queries along with their answers**.
- Moderators can **remove outdated or irrelevant queries**.

---

## Rating Professor Page

The Rating Professor page allows students to share feedback about professors while ensuring quality through moderation.

### User Features
- Users are shown a **list of available professors**.
- Users can **select a professor and submit a review**.
- Users can **upvote or downvote existing reviews**.

### Review Categories
- **Pending**: Reviews awaiting moderator approval.
- **Approved**: Reviews visible to all users and displayed at the top.
- **Not Approved**: Rejected reviews that are removed from public view.

### Moderator Features
- Moderators can **add/remove professors**.
- Moderators can **approve or reject reviews**.
- Pending reviews are **visible only to moderators**.
- Approved reviews are **prioritized in display order**.
- Not approved reviews are **removed from the system**.
- Moderators can **delete reviews** when necessary.
